"Would you like to talk about family planning today?

There are many effective methods that might work well for you to prevent or delay pregnancy. It is very important for the health of both a woman and her baby to delay pregnancy for at least 2 years after giving birth. And, if you have fulfilled desire to have babies , being on contraception helps you to prevent unwanted pregnancies. Women use family planning methods for many reasons. Some contraceptive methods also have additional benefits such as making your monthly bleeds less heavy.

If you are interested, I have different cards and contraception samples, which I can show you and discuss. First, I will ask you a few questions to help you decide which family planning methods might work best for you. I will discuss the methods that are available, the benefits, some of the possible side effects, and how you can access the methods. This is called balanced counselling and the counselling can help you to make decision on starting on a new contraception or change your current contraception. This will only take 5-10 minutes.""के तपाई आज परिवार नियोजनको बारेमा कुरा गर्न चाहनुहुन्छ ?
